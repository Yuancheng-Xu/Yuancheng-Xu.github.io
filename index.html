About me

My name is Yuancheng Xu (徐源诚) and I am a Research Scientist at Netflix's Eyeline Labs, where I develop foundational AI models with advanced multimodal understanding, reasoning, interaction, and generation capabilities. I earned my Ph.D. in 2025 from the University of Maryland, College Park, under the supervision of Furong Huang.
